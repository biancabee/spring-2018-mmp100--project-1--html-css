Jake Gyllenhaal

Jake Gyllenhaal by Nathan Congleton. Second photo taken by Dia Dipasupil/Getty Images.

Jacob Benjamin Gyllenhaal or more commonly known as Jake Gyllenhaal is a phenomenal American actor that immerses himself into his roles, no matter the character. He was born in Los Angeles, California. Gyllenhaal appeals to me not only because of his fantastic acting but also the kind personality he harbors, and also his interactions with others.
